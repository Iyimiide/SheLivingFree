About Us

She Living Free is dedicated to empowering women through various initiatives aimed at fostering independence, education, and professional growth. We believe in a world where every woman has the opportunity to live free and achieve her dreams.

Our Projects

Community Outreaches

"Our mission is to empower young people. We do this by providing sanitary products to girls in schools, so they can stay in class without worry. Additionally, we offer financial support to underprivileged students, helping them overcome barriers and achieve their dreams."

Tech Mentorship

"Our mission is to create a more inclusive tech industry. We offer support and resources to women who want to enter the field, helping to bridge the gender gap and foster a diverse and thriving community."

Business Support

"We're committed to empowering women entrepreneurs. Our programs provide support with business registrations and other essential resources, helping women start and grow successful businesses."

Pregnancy Support

"We're dedicated to supporting pregnant women throughout their journey. Our programs provide a range of resources, ensuring they have everything they need for a healthy and happy pregnancy."

Mentorship Programs

Our mentorship programs are designed to guide women through their professional and educational journeys, while also providing them with a community.

  • Ivy League MBA Preparation

    Receive personalized guidance and support as you prepare to apply to top-tier MBA programs, including Ivy League schools.

  • Tech Field Mentorship

    Connect with experienced mentors in the tech industry who can provide advice, networking opportunities, and career development guidance.

  • Entrepreneurship Guidance

    Gain valuable insights and practical advice from successful female entrepreneurs as you launch and grow your own business.
